01

The Thesis

Start with what funders want to fund. Research problems that match. Build evidence that survives scrutiny. Every claim verifiable, every statistic cited.

Capital-First

Traditional ventures seek funding after building. We start with funding criteria and work backwards to create ventures that are fundable by design.

Evidence-Backed

Every claim linked to a third-party source. Market sizing with citations. Competitive analysis that surfaces real competitors. Risks documented, not hidden.

AI-Driven Research

Deep research agents synthesize hundreds of sources into comprehensive, cited reports. What took weeks now takes hours.

Human Decisions

AI researches and generates. Humans review, approve, and make strategic decisions. Quality gates at every phase.

02

Five Phases to Funded

From funding criteria to funded venture. Build first, get traction, then fund. Funders want proof—we give them customers before asking for capital.

Phase 1: Research

Capital Thesis

What do funders want? What are their criteria?

Problem Thesis

Is this a real, documented problem?

Market Thesis

Is the market big enough? TAM/SAM/SOM.

Competitive Thesis

Who else is solving this? Where do we position?

Phase 2: Design

Solution Thesis

What do we build? Why will it win?

Business Thesis

How does it make money? Unit economics.

Risk Thesis

What could go wrong? Mitigations.

Phase 3: Materials

Evidence Library

Every claim verified. All sources accessible.

Pitch Materials

Deck, summary, applications. Consistent story.

Phase 4: Build

Architecture

Tech spec, data model, UI/UX design.

MVP

Working software. AI-accelerated development.

Phase 5: Traction & Fund

Customer Traction

LOI or first revenue. Proof funders need.

Funding Execution

Close funding with traction proof.
