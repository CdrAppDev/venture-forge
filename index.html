The Thesis

Start with what funders want to fund. Research problems that match. Build evidence that survives scrutiny. Every claim verifiable, every statistic cited.

Capital-First

Traditional ventures seek funding after building. We start with funding criteria and work backwards to create ventures that are fundable by design.

Evidence-Backed

Every claim linked to a third-party source. Market sizing with citations. Competitive analysis that surfaces real competitors. Risks documented, not hidden.

AI-Driven Research

Deep research agents synthesize hundreds of sources into comprehensive, cited reports. What took weeks now takes hours.

Human Decisions

AI researches and generates. Humans review, approve, and make strategic decisions. Quality gates at every phase.

03

The Agentic Machine

This isn't automation. It's autonomous AI agents that investigate, reason, and build— without human intervention. You give them an objective. They figure out how to achieve it.

Research Agent
Gemini Deep Research

You say: "Research the rural hospital claim denial problem."

The agent autonomously: plans a research strategy, executes 80-160 web searches, reads industry reports, synthesizes 40+ sources, resolves contradictions, and produces a 15-page cited report. In 30 minutes. Unsupervised.

↓
Reasoning Agent
Claude Opus 4.5

Takes 4 research reports (80+ pages, 150+ citations). Cross-references every claim. Identifies patterns, contradictions, gaps. Reconciles conflicting statistics. Outputs structured evidence with confidence ratings. Holds entire ventures in memory.

↓
Build Agent
Claude Code

Reads the spec. Plans the architecture. Writes production code. Creates tests. Sets up infrastructure. Deploys. Iterates on feedback. Software development as an autonomous process.

↓
Human
Strategic Decisions Only

"Is this problem worth solving?" "Is this positioning defensible?" "Ready to fund?" You make the calls. The machine does the work.

The multiplier: These agents run in parallel. While one investigates Opportunity A, others research B, C, D simultaneously. One operator. Dozens of ventures. The bottleneck isn't research or building—it's human judgment at decision points.
